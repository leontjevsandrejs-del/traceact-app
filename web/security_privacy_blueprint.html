Security & Data Privacy Blueprint

Local-First, Zero-Retention Architecture

The EU AI Act Compliance Auditor is engineered as a self-contained desktop application. Your compliance workspace — document ingestion, regulatory triage, and report assembly — executes entirely on your own hardware, inside your own network perimeter. PV Services operates no backend for this product: we cannot see, store, or retain your data, because it is never sent to us.

🔒 On-Device Processing

All document parsing (PDF and text extraction), risk-triage logic, and audit report generation run natively on the workstation. The embedded interface is served exclusively on the local loopback address (127.0.0.1) and is never exposed to the network.

🚫 Zero Vendor Server Contact

No PV Services cloud component exists in the data path. The application requires no account, no license activation server, and no registration. Uploaded evidence and audit results are never transmitted to, or retained on, PV Services infrastructure.

📊 No Telemetry or Tracking

Usage analytics and telemetry are disabled at build time. The application emits no behavioral tracking, crash reporting, or usage statistics to any party.

🔑 Customer-Held Credentials

The AI analysis credential (API key) is provisioned and held by your organization in a local configuration file on the workstation. You control the key, its rotation, and its revocation at all times.

🛡️ No Training-Loop Exposure

The product contains no data-collection pipeline of ours, and PV Services never uses customer content for model training. AI inference is performed via an enterprise API tier whose terms exclude the use of submitted content for model training (see disclosure below).

✅ Verifiable Distribution

Every installer release is published with its SHA-256 cryptographic checksum, enabling IT departments to verify binary integrity and whitelist the application before internal rollout.

Transparent Sub-Processor Disclosure — AI Analysis Step

The optional AI-assisted risk classification step transmits the text extracted from your uploaded evidence, together with your triage responses, to the Google Gemini API over TLS-encrypted channels for the sole purpose of generating your compliance assessment. This is the only moment any customer content leaves the workstation, it occurs only when you initiate an audit, and the response is returned directly to your local session.

Under the Gemini API paid-tier terms, submitted prompts are not used to train or improve Google models. Organizations with stricter data residency mandates can route this step through their own Google Cloud project and region, retaining full contractual control over the inference boundary.
